Where's the News?

Where's the news is an android application that eneabled users to search for free news articles based on location. Users could either use their current location or search for a specific location they had in mind. The application made use of a couple of free sources such as FireBase DB to store user information and google's free geolocator for location searching. This was a stand alone solo project and my first introduction to mobile app developmet. I would love to do more app development in the future. Unfortunatly I am an apple user so I would like to try IOS development next time.
